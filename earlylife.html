Early Life

I was born and raised in Rockland Maine where I have lived all my life. My parents are Tim and Angie Matero. I have 2 older brothers named Aaron and Ryan. As a child, my parents described me as a "wild child". Putting spaghetti on my head and causing mischief. My father was a professional bowler and as a child he introduced me to candlepin bowling. We would go to the bowling alley at least twice a week were he would teach me the proper form.
